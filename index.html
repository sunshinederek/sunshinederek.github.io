The Joy of Dancing!

Dancing has been a joy for me since I started school here at Fresno City College. Many times it was the only reason I wanted to come to school. I enjoy all types of dance styles.


Hip Hop

(Spring 2017)

My first and favorite dance class taken here at Fresno City College was Hip Hop, taken with instructor Cristal Tiscareno, who is a fanstastic dance instructor. This is class became an outlet for me when I was taking 15 units.

Modern Dance

(Fall 2017)

Although this was my second dance class and one of my least favorite dance styles, I met lots of great people, had loads of fun, and learned a great deal about dance. This class was also taken with Cristal Tiscareno.

Jazz Dance

(My Second Favorite)

I have not tried any Jazz dance classes, but I would love to. Jazz is my second favorite dance style. At one point I tried to register for a Jazz class with Cristal Tiscareno but sadly it did not fit with my class schedule.

Salsa Dance

(Leisure/Fun)

I currently attend Elephant Lounges Salsa Dance Nights most Fridays. It is something I do leisurely and for fun. I consider myself a decent salsa dancer but much improvement is needed.

African Dance

(A New Found Desire)

I've been recently exposed to a dance studio who offers African Dance classes. I've never been interested in African Dance, but after watching the students perform, I believe I've developed a love for this particular genre.
